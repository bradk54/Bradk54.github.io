About Me

Hello! I’m Brad Kittrell, a passionate and self-motivated data scientist with a rich background in the energy sector. My journey into data science is somewhat unconventional but deeply rewarding. Without the formal confines of a traditional degree in data science, my path has been carved out through real-world experiences, self-directed learning, and a relentless pursuit of understanding. Thats resulted in a wide array of experience in the field. In my professional work I have worked on projects ranging from classification, to regression and forecasting.
